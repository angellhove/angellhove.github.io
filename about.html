About Me

I am Angelica Joy Cantiveros, a bachelor of Science in Information Technology graduate. I started my career as a Freelance Web Designer since 2015 and right until now I worked as a part-time freelancer. As a freelancer, I always make sure that I was successful at presenting a creative design concept to my clients.

Currently, I worked as a Front-End Developer at one of the company here in Cebu Philippines. I collaborate with product managers, teams, and developers. We deliver designs that meet clients needs and also enhance the end users experience.

Being a front-end developer is my passion, I love creating web templates designs like PSD to HTML and PSD to Wordpress. I am an artistic yet practical UX designer.

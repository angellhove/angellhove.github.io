About Me

I am Angelica Joy Cantiveros, a bachelor of Science in Information Technology graduate. I started my career as a Freelance Web Designer since 2015 and right until now I worked as a part-time freelancer. As a freelancer, I always make sure that I was successful at presenting a creative design concept to my clients. Currently, I worked as a Front-End Developer at one of the company here in Cebu Philippines. I collaborate with product managers, teams, and developers. We deliver designs that meet clients needs and also enhance the end users experience. Being a front-end developer is my passion, I love creating web templates designs like PSD to HTML and PSD to Wordpress. I am an artistic yet practical UX designer.

Strategy

I am focus in collaborating with clients, managers and teams to nurture and transform ideas into well thought out design specs. After all, that's where the majority of amazing user experiences start.

UI/UX

I sketch and wireframe interfaces focusing on content structure, intuitive UI patterns and simple interactions. I usually used tools design like Adobe Photoshop, and Adobe Illustrator. I'm a minimalist who truly believes that less is more.

Coding

I design in the browser with HTML(5), CSS(3) and a touch of JavaScript with JQuery framework. I love coding things from scratch, but I can work with front-end frameworks like Bootstrap and MDB. For additional development of my design I usually used SAAS, LESS, JADE, and GULP JS. I am also an expert in creating a wordpress site.
